## Activation Targets Overview 
 Activation Targets in Salesforce Data Cloud are the destinations where you send your carefully built customer segments to be used in real-world marketing and engagement activities. Think of them as the 'launchpads' that bridge your unified customer data in Data Cloud with other systems like email platforms, advertising networks, or commerce sites. By configuring an Activation Target, you define how customer data from your segments is delivered to these external systems, enabling personalized customer experiences across various channels based on your audience definitions.
