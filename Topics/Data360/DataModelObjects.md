## Data Model Objects Overview 
 Data Model Objects (DMOs) are standardized structures within Salesforce Data Cloud that act as blueprints for organizing your customer data in a consistent and meaningful way. They allow you to map incoming information from various sources into a common, understandable format, such as 'Individual' for customers or 'Sales Order' for purchases. This standardization ensures that all your diverse customer data can be easily unified, analyzed, and activated for personalized customer experiences, regardless of its original source.
