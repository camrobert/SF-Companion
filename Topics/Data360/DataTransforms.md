## Data Transforms Overview 
 Data Transforms in Salesforce Data Cloud enable you to clean, combine, and prepare your customer data before it's used for segmentation and activation. This feature allows you to manipulate raw data from various sources by performing operations like filtering, joining, and calculating new values, ensuring your data is standardized and accurate. The process helps in creating a unified and reliable customer profile, which is crucial for effective personalization and targeted marketing strategies. 

## Related Topics
 Data Transforms UI, SQL Expressions, Joins (Left, Right, Inner, Full Outer), Filters, Aggregations, Projections, Derived Fields, Unions, Deduplication, Data Preparation Canvas, Data Model Objects (DMOs) as targets, Schema Mapping, Lookup operations, Data preview.
