## Data Lake Objects Overview 
Data Lake Objects (DLOs) are the foundational containers in Salesforce Data Cloud where all raw, ingested customer data is initially stored. When you set up data streams to bring information into Data Cloud, a corresponding Data Lake Object is automatically created to hold that specific dataset in its original format. These DLOs act as the staging ground, preserving the fidelity of your source data before it undergoes harmonization, segmentation, or activation for customer insights and engagement.
