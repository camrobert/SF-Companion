## Data Streams Overview 
Data Streams are the essential pipelines that bring all your valuable customer data into Salesforce Data Cloud. They act as connectors, pulling information from various sources like your Salesforce CRM, Marketing Cloud, website activity, or external databases, ensuring a comprehensive view of every customer interaction. Once configured, Data Streams continuously ingest this information, automatically transforming and organizing it into a unified profile that powers personalized experiences across your customer journey.
