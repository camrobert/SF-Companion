## Data Shares Overview 
 Data Shares is a powerful feature within Salesforce Data Cloud that allows you to securely share your unified customer data, including segments and calculated insights, with various internal and external systems. It works by activating processed data from Data Cloud and making it accessible to target platforms like Salesforce CRM, Marketing Cloud, or external data warehouses. This ensures a consistent view of your customers across all your business applications, enabling personalized experiences and informed decision-making.
