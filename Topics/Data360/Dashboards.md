## Dashboards Overview 
 Salesforce Data Cloud Dashboards provide a powerful visual interface to understand your unified customer data and gain actionable insights. Leveraging CRM Analytics, these dashboards allow users to combine various data points from customer profiles, engagement history, and marketing campaigns into interactive charts and tables. This enables businesses to monitor key performance indicators, identify trends, and make data-driven decisions regarding customer experience and marketing strategies.
