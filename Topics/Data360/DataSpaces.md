## Data Spaces Overview 
 Data Spaces in Salesforce Data Cloud act like secure, walled-off compartments for your data. They allow different business units or teams within an organization to manage their own customer data, profiles, and engagement activations independently, ensuring data isolation and adherence to specific compliance requirements. By segmenting data this way, you can tailor your marketing, sales, and service efforts to specific audiences while maintaining strict control over who can access and use particular datasets.
