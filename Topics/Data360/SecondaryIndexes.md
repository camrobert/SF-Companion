## Secondary Indexes Overview 
 Secondary Indexes in Salesforce Data Cloud are like extra signposts you add to your data to make it much faster to find specific information. They help the system quickly locate records based on values in certain fields, without having to scan through an entire dataset. This greatly improves the performance of queries, segmentation, and activation processes, especially when you frequently filter or join data using these specific fields.
