## Data Share Targets Overview 
 Data Share Targets in Salesforce Data Cloud allow you to precisely send your enriched customer data and defined audience segments to various external platforms. This feature acts as the bridge, ensuring the powerful insights you build within Data Cloud can be utilized by your marketing, advertising, and analytics systems. By configuring a target, you enable automated export of unified customer profiles or segment memberships, making your data actionable across your entire technology stack.
