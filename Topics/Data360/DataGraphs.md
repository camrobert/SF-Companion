## Data Graphs Overview 
 Data Graphs in Salesforce Data Cloud allow you to connect and combine various pieces of customer information from different data sources, creating a comprehensive and unified view. This feature works by defining specific relationships between your data streams and objects, which then link attributes together to enrich your unified customer profiles. By understanding these connections, marketers and analysts can gain deeper insights, build more precise segments, and deliver highly personalized experiences.
