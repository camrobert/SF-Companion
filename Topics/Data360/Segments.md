## Segments Overview 
 Segments in Salesforce Data Cloud allow you to group your individual customers based on specific criteria derived from all your connected data sources. By combining customer behaviors, preferences, and attributes into meaningful groups, you can understand your audience better and tailor unique experiences. These precisely defined customer groups are then used to power targeted marketing campaigns, personalized content, and relevant customer journeys across various activation platforms.
