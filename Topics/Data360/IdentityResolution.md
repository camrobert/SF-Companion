## Identity Resolution Overview 
 Identity Resolution in Salesforce Data Cloud is a powerful feature that takes fragmented customer information from all your different systems, like website visits, purchases, and marketing interactions. It intelligently matches and combines these scattered pieces of data, even if they use varying identifiers, into a single, comprehensive customer profile. This unified view helps businesses understand each customer uniquely and consistently, enabling highly personalized experiences across every touchpoint.
